My Reasons for Learning to Code!

  • My Family
  • Quality of Life
  • Myself

My Family

Coding is going to help my family life. My husband is the main provider in our family and works a commission only position. My current job does I do not bring in enough income to provide for the family other then daycare costs. By learning to code and obtaining a position in a development role will allow me to provide for my family and support my husband so he does not have to stress so much over money. Also it will allow me to provide for my son and my baby I am currently pregnant with.

Quality of Life

Learning to code and starting a new job where I will be bringing in substantially more income will allow for my qulaity of life to improve because I will be able to enjoy life more and participate in more fun activities with my family. I will be able to bring my kids out on fun adventures and take more time with my family on vacations and doing fun events together. We won't have to worry about household bills as much and be able to have some savings set aside in case of emergency.

Myself

My degree is in Resort and Hospitality management and had much more pride in myself when I was in a higher paying position as a hotel manager prior to having my son and becoming a single mom. Now I am working for much less income and I know I am capable of so much more. I have always enjoyed math and science and have loved computers, my father is a developer for SalesForce.com so I grew up around him and seeing him enjoy his job and the benefits of his line of work have attracted me to learn coding so I can better myself. I am so excited for this opportunity to better myself and apply the knowledge to a skill I feel I am capable of learning and doing.
